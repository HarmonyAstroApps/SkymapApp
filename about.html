About Skymap

We built Skymap because exploring the night sky should be accessible to everyone, not just astronomers with expensive telescopes.

Skymap is an iOS astronomy app that uses augmented reality to help you identify stars, planets, constellations, and celestial objects in real-time. Point your device at the sky, and instantly discover what you're looking at.

Why We Built Skymap

We believe that the universe should be accessible to curious minds. Whether you're a student learning about constellations, a teacher bringing astronomy to life in the classroom, a hobbyist stargazer, or just someone who's ever looked up and wondered what's out there — Skymap is for you.

Traditional stargazing requires equipment, training, and patience. Skymap removes those barriers. Your iPhone becomes your personal planetarium.

Our Mission

To make astronomy accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone. We focus on accurate data, beautiful design, and a frictionless experience so you can spend your time exploring the universe, not fighting the app.

What Makes Skymap Different

  • Real-time AR Tracking — Instant identification with scientific accuracy
  • Constellation Stories — Learn mythology and history behind the stars
  • 88 Constellations — Complete coverage of the night sky
  • Works Offline — No internet needed for stargazing
  • Educational Content — Perfect for students and teachers
  • Beautiful Design — Optimized for dark skies and low light
