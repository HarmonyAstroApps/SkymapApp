Honest Answers

Quick questions.

Do I need internet to use it?

No. After the initial download, Skymap works fully offline. Your GPS and device sensors do all the heavy lifting locally. Perfect for camping, flights, or any place without signal.

How accurate is it?

Skymap uses your iPhone's GPS, compass, and gyroscope plus a precomputed ephemeris of planet and star positions. Position accuracy is within ±1° on a calibrated iPhone — well within naked-eye precision.

Will it drain my battery?

Sky mode uses the camera and sensors, so battery use is moderate. We include a "list mode" without AR for quick lookups that's near-zero impact.

Is it really free?

Yes. The full app is free with no ads. We don't sell data and there's no subscription. If you love it, leaving a review is the only "thank you" we ask for.
