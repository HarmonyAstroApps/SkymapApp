Create a video showcasing any HarmonyAstroApps app — a stargazing timelapse with Skymap, a reaction time challenge with Cowboy Quick Shot, or anything creative you can think of. The rules

  • Your post must include an accessible link to the app — in your bio, video description, or pinned comment. Viewers should be able to find and download the app within one tap.
  • Your post must remain live for the entire duration of your reward. If you delete it, the premium gets revoked.
  • Rewards are based on the highest verified view count within 30 days of your first submission. You can resubmit for a higher tier during that window.
  • One reward per post. You earn the highest tier your video qualifies for, not a combination of tiers.
  • Content must be original and feature one of our apps. The content style is entirely up to you.

FAQ

Can I submit the same post multiple times?

Yes. If your video grows past the next tier within 30 days of your first submission, resubmit the form and we'll upgrade your reward to the higher tier. After 30 days, the tier is locked based on your view count at that time.

Which apps are eligible?

Any app by HarmonyAstroApps — Skymap, Cowboy Quick Shot, Flight Mode Chat, Voice Calculator, Voice Clock, or Batchly.

Where should the app link go?

Wherever your platform allows clickable links — typically your bio (TikTok, Instagram) or the video description (YouTube). A pinned comment also works.
